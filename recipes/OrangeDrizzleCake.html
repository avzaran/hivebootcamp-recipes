Orange Drizzle Cake

Cake. Citrus. One-bowl baking. If these words send a shiver down your spine, then this recipe is for you.
It is a very easy recipe that can be different every time you make it if you swap the oranges for lemons, limes, grapefruit, or a mix of juices. You get a not-too-sweet, moist cake that will be gone before the day is over.
Remember to use exact measurements to get perfect results.

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Additional Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 45 mins Servings: 8 Yield: 1 9-inch cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup self-rising flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • ⅔ cup superfine sugar
  • ⅔ cup salted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Drizzle

  • ½ cup superfine sugar
  • ¼ cup orange juice
