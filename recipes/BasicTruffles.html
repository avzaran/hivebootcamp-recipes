Basic Truffles

This truffle recipe makes a delicious chocolate ganache filling to which you can add your own flavorings or extracts.
You may use this to fill the chocolate shells you make using candy molds or roll into balls then into powdered sugar, cocoa, festive sprinkles or other toppings.

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Additional Time: 1 hr 30 mins
Total Time: 1 hrs 50 mins Servings: 35 Yield: 35 chocolate truffles

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  • ⅓ cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
